Federal Funding at Risk

EDA — Maritime Blue's historical federal backer — now states that past awards "do not necessarily reflect current investment priorities." Maritime Blue's Build to Scale rounds ($1.1M, 2020–2022) and the 2023 Venture Challenge award are under pressure. Note: Maritime Blue was a Phase 1 finalist in Build Back Better ($500K planning grant, 2021) but did not win Phase 2. Federal grants represent a significant share of historical operational funding and require strategic replacement via private philanthropy.

💡 Strategic Insight: The Philanthropic Gap
Current Reality

Maritime Blue's funding is 47% federal (at risk), 40%+ public sector, and only ~2% private philanthropy. Zero major gifts from individuals. Zero corporate philanthropy.

The Opportunity

$480M Packard Foundation ocean commitment, $250M Walton Family Foundation ocean fund, and $10B Bezos Earth Fund are actively looking for exactly what Maritime Blue does — and none of them are current funders.

The Development Role

The first Development Manager needs to build the philanthropic pipeline from near-zero, manage the $10M Maritime Forward campaign, and diversify away from federal dependency. The timing is urgent.
