The Maritime Blue Story in Numbers

Since 2020, Maritime Blue has built the Pacific Northwest's leading Blue Economy innovation cluster. The accelerator has invested ~$3.8M in 38 companies; alumni have collectively raised $300M+ — a ~79× multiplier on capital deployed. 400+ jobs created. The $33M Maritime Innovation Center (Port of Seattle + WA State capital investment, Maritime Blue anchor tenant) opens Summer 2026, cementing Washington's position as a global blue economy hub.

The Philanthropic Opportunity Gap

The global ocean philanthropy market has more than doubled in the past decade — reaching $1.2B/year by 2020 and growing rapidly. Blue tech VC hit $2.4B in 2024. The largest ocean foundations in the world — Packard, Moore, Walton, Bezos — are actively deploying hundreds of millions into exactly the space Maritime Blue operates. Yet none of them are currently Maritime Blue funders. This represents the single largest untapped opportunity for the organization's financial sustainability.

Strategic Framing

These prospects are identified based on three matching criteria: (1) mission alignment with ocean/marine/blue economy innovation, (2) Pacific Northwest presence or explicit interest, and (3) evidence of funding organizations similar to Maritime Blue's model. All prospects have zero current relationship — representing greenfield cultivation opportunities.
